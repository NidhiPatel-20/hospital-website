Facility

AYU-CARE Hospital is a 200 Bed Multi-Speciality Hospital, offering almost all major Medical Services. The Hospital has OPD and over 100 Doctors provide honorarium services. The 200 Bed Facility includes Centrally AC General Ward, Semi-Special Ward (AC and non-AC), Special Ward (AC and non-AC), Deluxe Ward and Super Deluxe Wards. The Hospital has a full-fledged Emergency Department, ICU facility, Radiology Department, Pathology Department, Kidney Dialysis, Health Check Up Department, Blood Bank, Physiotherapy Department etc. We have 4 Ultra Modern Operation Theatres where major surgeries including Joint Replacement, Kidney Transplant, Onco Surgery, Trauma, Urology etc are carried out.
